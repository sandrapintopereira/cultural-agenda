ABOUT

A cultural agenda built from the inside.

The project

Cultural Agenda was built as part of the Practical Laboratories module at Upskill's ServiceNow course. But the idea behind it goes further than an academic exercise.

With a background in languages and culture, and having been part of a collective in Braga whose mission was to connect artists, cultural workers, and venues — one of the things we were always asked for was a shared cultural agenda. A single place to find out what was happening, who was behind it, and how to get involved.

When this project brief was presented, choosing this theme was an easy decision. It made sense on every level.

What it is

A platform where individuals, artists, collectives, and venues can publish and discover cultural events — concerts, exhibitions, theatre, readings, and more. Anyone can explore the agenda. Those with an account can mark attendance, manage their own events, and build a public profile.

Where it's going

The vision is for this to grow into a proper cultural platform — a space where artists and collectives can be found, where venues can be mapped, and where cultural life in a city becomes visible and accessible to everyone.
